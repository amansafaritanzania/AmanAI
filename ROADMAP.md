# 🗺️ Aman AI Roadmap

## Vision

Build a Tanzania-focused AI platform that helps people learn, farm, create, and solve problems through specialised expert systems.

Aman AI will grow from a chatbot into a trusted digital assistant for education, agriculture and everyday life.
